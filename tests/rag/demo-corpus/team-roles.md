# Team Roles

## Engineering Lead

Maya Patel is the engineering lead. She owns the technical roadmap, makes final
architecture decisions, and conducts quarterly performance reviews for all engineers.
Maya approves all changes to the core review engine and the database schema. She can
be reached at maya@codebridge.io.

## Product Manager

James Okafor is the product manager. He writes feature specifications, prioritises the
backlog, and is the primary contact for enterprise customers requesting custom
integrations. James runs the weekly roadmap sync every Tuesday at 10:00 UTC.

## Backend Engineers

The backend team consists of three engineers: Priya Nair, Tom Weiss, and Lena Brandt.
They own the API layer, the diff-scoring pipeline, and the PostgreSQL schema. On-call
rotation cycles through Priya, Tom, and Lena on a weekly basis.

## Frontend Engineers

Kenji Watanabe and Sofia Reyes own the React frontend, the design system, and browser
extension integrations. They work closely with James to ship UI features and run
usability sessions with beta customers.

## DevOps / Infrastructure

Carlos Mendes is the sole infrastructure engineer. He manages the Kubernetes cluster,
CI/CD pipelines, and the self-hosted release process. Carlos is the only person with
production deploy access.
